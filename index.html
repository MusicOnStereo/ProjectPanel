CSRF Tokens

CSRF tokens are pieces of data sent from the server that verify identities on Scratch. CSRF tokens are tied to their respective accounts, and actions performed by an account are verified by its CSRF token. It is important these CSRF tokens are kept secret, since with an exposed CSRF token you are vulnerable to a CSRF attack.

This program is run locally, and your CSRF token isn't shared outside your device when using this program.
